Crawler Information

SandstormClaimsAuditor

An automated reader operated by Sandstorm Digital as part of the Claims Auditor tool.

User-Agent
SandstormClaimsAuditor/1.0 (+https://claims-auditor.sandstormdigital.com/bot)

What it does

This crawler reads the publicly accessible pages of websites we audit and checks the content for unsubstantiated claims, mapping each finding to Google's E-E-A-T quality framework so site owners can strengthen credibility, search performance, and visibility in AI assistants.

How it behaves

  • Read-only. It only reads page content. It never submits forms, logs in, makes purchases, or changes anything on your site.
  • Public pages only. It reads the same pages any ordinary visitor can see.
  • Low volume. It runs on request when a site owner or their agency initiates an audit, not on a continuous schedule.
  • Identifiable. Every request carries the user-agent shown above so it is easy to recognize in your logs.

Allowlisting our crawler

If you operate a site we are auditing and your bot protection is blocking us, we can supply a private token so you can allowlist only our crawler. When authorized, our requests carry a private header named X-Sandstorm-Auth holding a secret token shared with you directly. You can add a single rule that lets through only requests carrying that token, without opening your site to anyone else.
